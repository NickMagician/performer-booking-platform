About Ben

With over 10 years of professional experience, Ben Williams has established himself as one of Manchester's most sought-after close-up magicians. His unique blend of classic sleight-of-hand techniques and modern psychological illusions creates unforgettable moments that leave audiences questioning the very nature of reality.

Ben's journey into magic began at the age of 12 when he received his first magic set as a birthday gift. What started as a childhood fascination quickly evolved into a lifelong passion and eventually a thriving career. After studying Psychology at the University of Manchester, Ben combined his understanding of human behavior with his magical skills to create performances that are not just tricks, but genuine experiences of wonder.

What sets Ben apart is his ability to adapt his performance to any audience and setting. Whether he's mingling with guests during a wedding cocktail hour, breaking the ice at a corporate networking event, or entertaining children at a birthday party, Ben's warm personality and professional approach ensure that every performance is perfectly tailored to the occasion.
